==========================================================
NETWORK DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR FUTURE GROWTH (HLD/LLD)
==========================================================

1. HIERARCHICAL DESIGN (3-LAYER MODEL)
--------------------------------------
- Core: Optimized for high-speed switching only.
- Distribution: Policy-based connectivity and aggregation.
- Access: Layer 2/3 connectivity for end devices.
* WHY: Allows you to add access switches without impacting the core.

2. IP ADDRESSING & SUBNETTING
-----------------------------
- Always reserve 30% overhead for future hosts.
- Use Summarizable IP blocks (Contiguous addresses).
* WHY: Keeps Routing Tables small and efficient as you grow.

3. PHYSICAL LAYER SCALABILITY
-----------------------------
- Use Fiber-Optic (SFP+/QSFP+) for inter-switch links.
- Implement Switch Stacking (Cisco StackWise).
* WHY: To increase port density, just "stack" another switch.

4. SD-WAN & CLOUD READINESS
---------------------------
- Ensure hardware supports Telemetry and API-driven config.
- Design Internet Edge with HA (High Availability).
* WHY: Seamless migration to Cloud services in the future.
